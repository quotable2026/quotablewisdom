Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Quotable Wisdom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Email address (optional, provided voluntarily for app updates and notifications)
  • Subscription and purchase information (processed by Apple)
  • Feedback and support requests you send us

Automatically Collected Information:

  • App usage data (which features you use, quotes you favorite)
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • Language preferences
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Process subscriptions and purchases
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send important updates about the app (if you've provided your email)
  • Translate quotes into your preferred language

4. Email Collection

When you choose to provide your email address within the Quotable app to receive notifications about new features and updates, we collect and store your email address using EmailOctopus, a third-party email service provider.

Information Collected:

  • Email address (provided voluntarily by you)

How We Use It:

  • Send you notifications about app updates, new features, and new quote collections
  • Communication is optional and you can unsubscribe at any time

Third-Party Service:

  • Your email is stored and managed by EmailOctopus (emailoctopus.com)
  • EmailOctopus's privacy policy: https://emailoctopus.com/legal/privacy

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with any other third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage

Your favorites, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not store this information on our servers.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and payment processing
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing. RevenueCat may collect information about your device, subscription status, and purchase history to provide subscription services. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy
  • EmailOctopus: For email collection and communication (optional)
  • Google Cloud Translation API: For translating quotes into your preferred language. Quote text is sent to Google's servers for translation. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal information to use basic features.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Opt out of email communications (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Request information about data we collect
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (through iOS settings)

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and, if you've provided your email, by sending you a notification.
