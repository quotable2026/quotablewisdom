Refund Policy

Purchases Made Through Apple App Store

All purchases made through the Quotable app, including Premium subscriptions and Lifetime access, are processed and managed by Apple through the App Store. As such, all refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple.

How to Request a Refund

Quotable does not process refunds directly. If you'd like to request a refund for a purchase made through the App Store, please follow these steps:

Option 1: Through Apple's Website

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Select "Request a refund"
  4. Choose the reason for your refund request
  5. Select the Quotable purchase you want to refund
  6. Submit your request
Request Refund from Apple

Option 2: Through Your Device

  1. Open the App Store app
  2. Tap your profile picture at the top
  3. Tap "Purchased"
  4. Find Quotable in your purchase history
  5. Tap "Report a Problem"
  6. Follow the prompts to request a refund

Refund Eligibility

Apple typically reviews refund requests within 48 hours. Refund eligibility is determined by Apple based on their policies and your purchase history. For more information about Apple's refund policy, please visit:

Important Notes

Before Requesting a Refund:

If you're having technical issues with the app, please contact us at support@quotablewisdom.app before requesting a refund. We're here to help and many issues can be quickly resolved!

  • Refunds are not guaranteed and are granted at Apple's discretion
  • If your refund is approved, you may lose access to Premium features
  • Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to appear in your account
  • Multiple refund requests for the same purchase won't speed up the process
