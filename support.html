How does the free tier work?

You start with 500 carefully curated quotes. Over the next 9 months, you'll gradually unlock up to 2,000 quotes. All core features are included in the free tier.

What's the difference between Annual and Lifetime Premium?

Annual Premium ($6.99/year): Access all 7,202 quotes with early access to new content. Renews annually.

Lifetime Premium ($19.99 one-time): Pay once and own all quotes forever, including all future updates.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select Quotable Wisdom
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Quotable Wisdom purchase
  4. Select "Request a refund"

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All quotes are stored locally on your device, so you can access them anytime without an internet connection.
